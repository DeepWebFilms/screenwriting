About the Creator

Stephen Gaffney is an Irish filmmaker and screenwriter with over a decade of experience telling stories for film, television, and documentary. He holds a BA degree in filmmaking from the New York Film Academy and has been actively working in independent production since graduating.

His work spans psychological thrillers, gritty crime dramas, and real-world documentary — with an emphasis on character, realism, and emotional tension. Stephen believes that writing should always be grounded in what can be made, and that the best scripts are often the simplest ones done well.

Selected Work

  • Red Room – A psychological thriller praised for its raw intensity
  • Class A – A grounded crime film exploring Ireland’s underworld
  • The Kinahan Cartel – A feature documentary on one of Ireland’s most notorious criminal networks
  • McGregor Forever (Netflix) – Served as Supervising Editor on the acclaimed sports documentary series
