What You'll Learn

A practical, straightforward course that focuses on writing scripts that can actually be made. Built from real experience in the Irish and international film industries.

  • How to write with production in mind
  • Building grounded, believable characters
  • Understanding story structure without relying on formulas
  • Writing in all genres
  • How to approach rewriting
  • Live Zoom Q&A after completing the course

About the Instructor

Stephen Gaffney is an Irish filmmaker with credits including Red Room, Class A, The Kinahan Cartel, and Netflix’s McGregor Forever. His approach to screenwriting is grounded in real-world application, with a focus on clarity, realism, and emotional impact.

Course Format

The course includes 6 written modules in PDF format. At the end, you'll have access to a live Zoom session where you can ask questions and get feedback.
