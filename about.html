About Stephen Gaffney

Stephen Gaffney is an Irish filmmaker known for his commitment to ultra-realistic, emotionally raw storytelling. His work spans film, documentary, and television — with titles such as Red Room, Class A, The Kinahan Cartel, and Netflix’s McGregor Forever.

Stephen founded Deep Web Films and has become known for tackling difficult subjects with unflinching honesty. This screenwriting course is a reflection of that same ethos — no fluff, just the essential tools to write a script that can be made.

To learn more about Stephen’s work, visit stephengaffneyfilm.com or his IMDb page.
