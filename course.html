Full Course Breakdown

Six practical modules designed to help you write better scripts, faster.

Module 1: What Is a Screenplay?

Understanding what a screenplay really is, how it differs from other writing, and how to think visually from the start.

Module 2: Ideas and Subject

How to find strong ideas, develop them into concepts, and decide what’s worth turning into a script.

Module 3: Character

How to build believable characters with goals, contradictions, and emotional impact.

Module 4: Structure

A practical guide to story structure that doesn’t rely on rigid formulas, using setups, turning points, and momentum.

Module 5: Dialogue and Rewriting

Writing authentic dialogue and improving your script through editing and cutting.

Module 6: Getting It Made

Writing for production: how to finish, pitch, and move toward actually shooting your work. Plus a live Zoom session to wrap up.
